Golden Pinky

F3 Austin Nantan, since 2020.

Golden Pinky has been a part of F3 Nation since November, 2017. He started in F3 Churham and spent over half a year being transformed by some mighty HIMs. After moving back home to Austin, TX, he launched a region in North Austin (NATX) and many men joined in on the fun! In 2020, COVID allowed the group the chance to unite with F3 Austin and form a super region, focused on bringing F3 to all the men in the Greater Austin area!
